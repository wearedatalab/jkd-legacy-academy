How Admission Works

This Is Not a
Drop-In Gym.

The JKD Legacy Academy is a private membership Kwoon. We don't take walk-ins. The path begins with a single inquiry.

Step 01

Inquire

Submit your form

Tell us about yourself, your background, and what draws you to Jeet Kune Do. The more honest, the better the conversation that follows.

Step 02

Conversation

A call with leadership

Sigung Vargas (or the Adelaide head instructor) personally calls every prospective student. Expect a real conversation about the path, the commitment, and the fit.

Step 03

Step on the Mat

Begin your foundation

If the fit is right on both sides, you'll be invited to begin the 12-week Foundation. Your journey into the lineage starts here.
